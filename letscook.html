Tofu Scramble Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped spinach, mushrooms, tomatoes, or any other vegetables you like
  • Optional: nutritional yeast for added flavor

Instructions:

  1. Press the tofu: Wrap the block of tofu in a clean kitchen towel or paper towels, place it on a plate, and put something heavy on top (like a skillet or a few heavy books). Let it press for about 15-20 minutes to remove excess moisture.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and bell pepper, and saut√© for 3-4 minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Crumble the pressed tofu into the skillet with your hands or a fork, resembling scrambled eggs.
  4. Add the minced garlic, turmeric, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to evenly distribute the spices and cook for another 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tofu is heated through and slightly browned.
  5. If you're adding any extra vegetables like spinach, mushrooms, or tomatoes, add them to the skillet now and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until they are softened.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. If you like, sprinkle some nutritional yeast over the scramble for a cheesy flavor.
  7. Once everything is heated through and well combined, remove the skillet from the heat and serve your tofu scramble hot.
  8. Enjoy your delicious and protein-packed tofu scramble! It's perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even a quick and healthy dinner option.

Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any plant-based milk of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed meal
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan protein powder (such as pea protein, soy protein, or brown rice protein)
  • Coconut oil or cooking spray, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Batter:
    • In a blender or food processor, combine the rolled oats, banana, almond milk, flaxseed meal, maple syrup (if using), vanilla extract, baking powder, cinnamon (if using), and salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
    • Transfer the mixture to a mixing bowl.
  2. Add Protein Powder:
    • Add the vegan protein powder to the pancake batter and stir until well combined. Adjust the consistency by adding a little more almond milk if the batter is too thick.
  3. Heat the Pan:
    • Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with coconut oil or cooking spray.
  4. Cook the Pancakes:
    • Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  5. Serve:
    • Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as sliced bananas, berries, nut butter, or a drizzle of maple syrup.

Besan Chilla Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Cooking oil or ghee, for cooking

Instructions:

Prepare the Batter:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the besan (gram flour), chopped onion, tomato, green chili, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Gradually add water to the besan mixture, stirring continuously, until you achieve a smooth and pourable batter consistency. The batter should not be too thick or too thin.
  3. Allow the batter to rest for about 10-15 minutes. This allows the flavors to meld together and the besan to absorb the water.

Cook the Chillas:

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. You can lightly grease the surface with oil if needed, although it's not always necessary.
  2. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the center of the hot skillet. Using the back of the ladle, spread the batter in a circular motion to form a thin pancake (chilla).
  3. Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges of the chilla to help it cook and become crisp.
  4. Cook the chilla for 2-3 minutes on one side until golden brown and crisp. Then, carefully flip it over and cook for another 2-3 minutes on the other side.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, making as many chillas as desired.

Serve:

Serve the besan chillas hot with your favorite chutney, yogurt, or pickle on the side.

Black Bean Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Black Bean Filling:

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For Assembling the Burrito:

  • Large whole wheat or gluten-free tortillas
  • Avocado slices
  • Fresh salsa or pico de gallo
  • Vegan cheese, shredded (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

Prepare the Black Bean Filling:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add diced onion and bell pepper, and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add minced garlic, ground cumin, and chili powder to the skillet. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the drained black beans to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for a few minutes until heated through and the flavors are well combined. Mash some of the black beans with the back of a fork for a thicker consistency if desired. Remove from heat.

Assemble the Burritos:

  1. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable.
  2. Place a generous spoonful of the black bean filling in the center of each tortilla.
  3. Add avocado slices, fresh salsa or pico de gallo, vegan cheese (if using), and chopped cilantro on top of the black bean filling.

Fold the Burritos:

  1. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward, then roll it up tightly from the bottom to form a burrito.

Serve:

Serve the vegan black bean breakfast burritos immediately, with hot sauce on the side if desired.

Optional:

You can also wrap the assembled burritos in foil and store them in the refrigerator for later. To reheat, simply place them in a preheated oven at 350¬∞F (175¬∞C) for about 10-15 minutes until heated through.

Overnight Oats Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats (old-fashioned oats)
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (or dairy-free yogurt for vegan option)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional, for added thickness and nutrition)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional, for flavor)

Toppings (Optional):

  • Sliced fruits (bananas, berries, apples, etc.)
  • Nuts and seeds (almonds, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, etc.)
  • Nut butter or seed butter
  • Shredded coconut
  • Dried fruits (raisins, cranberries, etc.)
  • Cinnamon or other spices

Instructions:

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a jar or container with a lid, combine the rolled oats, milk, Greek yogurt, chia seeds (if using), honey or maple syrup (if using), and vanilla extract (if using). Stir well until all ingredients are fully combined.
  2. Refrigerate Overnight: Cover the jar or container with a lid and refrigerate overnight, or for at least 4-6 hours. This allows the oats to soak up the liquid and soften.
  3. Add Toppings: The next morning, give the overnight oats a good stir. If the consistency is too thick, you can add a splash of milk to loosen it up. Add your desired toppings, such as sliced fruits, nuts, seeds, nut butter, shredded coconut, dried fruits, or spices. Be creative and customize your oats to your liking!
  4. Enjoy: Enjoy your delicious and nutritious overnight oats straight from the jar or container. They can be enjoyed cold, or you can heat them up in the microwave for a warm breakfast on colder days.

Tips:

  • You can make a batch of overnight oats ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. This makes for a convenient and quick breakfast option during busy mornings.
  • Feel free to experiment with different flavor combinations and toppings to keep your breakfasts exciting and varied.
  • Adjust the sweetness and thickness of the overnight oats according to your preference by adding more or less honey/maple syrup and milk.
  • Enjoy your creamy and satisfying overnight oats for a nutritious start to your day!

Chia Seed Pudding Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup chia seeds
  • 2 cups milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey, maple syrup, or sweetener of choice (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Toppings (Optional):

  • Nuts and seeds (almonds, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, etc.)
  • Granola or muesli
  • Shredded coconut
  • Honey or maple syrup for drizzling
  • Cinnamon or cocoa powder for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a bowl or jar, combine the chia seeds, milk, honey or sweetener (if using), and vanilla extract (if using). Stir well until all ingredients are evenly mixed.
  2. Let it Sit: Cover the bowl or jar and refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours, or preferably overnight. During this time, the chia seeds will absorb the liquid and thicken to create a pudding-like consistency.
  3. Stir and Serve: Once the chia pudding has reached the desired consistency, give it a good stir. If it's too thick, you can add a little more milk to adjust the consistency.
  4. Add Toppings: Serve the chia seed pudding in individual bowls or jars, and add your favorite toppings such as sliced fruits, nuts, seeds, granola, shredded coconut, honey, or spices like cinnamon or cocoa powder.
  5. Enjoy: Enjoy your delicious and nutritious chia seed pudding as a healthy breakfast, snack, or dessert!

Tips:

  • Feel free to adjust the sweetness of the pudding by adding more or less honey, maple syrup, or sweetener according to your preference.
  • Experiment with different flavors by adding ingredients like cocoa powder, matcha powder, or flavored extracts such as almond or coconut.
  • Chia seed pudding can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days, making it a great option for meal prep.

  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder

  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Finely chop the onion, tomato, and green chilies.
  2. Scramble Eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs well until smooth.
  3. Saut√©: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.

  6. Add Eggs: Pour beaten eggs into the pan. Stir continuously to scramble the eggs and mix them with the masala.
  7. Cook: Cook until the eggs are fully cooked and the mixture is dry to your desired consistency.
  8. Garnish: Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  9. Serve: Serve hot with roti, bread, or rice.

Tips:

Mutton Kebab Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams mutton (boneless), minced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Mutton: In a mixing bowl, combine minced mutton, chopped onion, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, fresh coriander leaves, fresh mint leaves, garam masala powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well to combine.
  2. Marinate: Cover the bowl and let the mutton mixture marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together.
  3. Shape the Kebabs: After marinating, take portions of the mutton mixture and shape them onto skewers to form kebabs. Press the mixture firmly onto the skewers to ensure they hold their shape during grilling.
  4. Grill: Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Brush the surface with cooking oil to prevent sticking. Place the mutton kebabs on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until they are fully cooked and have charred grill marks.
  5. Serve: Once cooked, remove the mutton kebabs from the grill and serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or chutney.

Tips:

  • For a leaner option, you can use lean mutton cuts or remove excess fat from the minced mutton before marinating.
  • If using wooden skewers, remember to soak them in water for at least 30 minutes before threading the mutton mixture to prevent them from burning during grilling.
  • You can adjust the spice levels according to your preference by adding more or less red chili powder and green chilies.
  • Feel free to serve the mutton kebabs with naan bread, rice, or salad for a complete meal.

Egg Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and halved
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Peel and halve the hard-boiled eggs. Finely chop the onion, tomatoes, and green chili. Mince the garlic and grate the ginger.
  2. Saut√© Aromatics: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onion, garlic, ginger, and green chili. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  3. Add Spices: Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala powder to the pan. Stir well and cook for a minute until the spices are fragrant.
  4. Cook Tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes to the pan. Cook until tomatoes are soft and the mixture thickens.
  5. Simmer: Add a little water if the mixture is too dry. Season with salt and let the curry simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Add Eggs: Gently add the halved hard-boiled eggs to the curry. Spoon the curry over the eggs to coat them evenly.
  7. Finish: Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the eggs are heated through and the curry reaches your desired consistency.
  8. Garnish: Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  9. Serve: Serve the healthy egg curry hot with rice, roti, or bread.

Tips:

  • To reduce the calorie content, you can use a non-stick pan and minimize the amount of oil used for saut√©ing.
  • For a creamier texture, you can add a tablespoon of yogurt or coconut milk to the curry.
  • Feel free to adjust the spice levels according to your preference by increasing or decreasing the amount of red chili powder and green chilies.
  • Adding vegetables like bell peppers, peas, or spinach can increase the nutritional value of the curry.

Chicken Tikka Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt, for serving
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate Chicken: In a bowl, mix together yogurt, tikka masala paste, lemon juice, ground cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Add chicken breasts to the marinade, turning to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best flavor.
  2. Cook Chicken: Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Brush chicken breasts with olive oil and grill for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and juices run clear. Remove from heat and let rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  3. Assemble Salad: In a large bowl, combine mixed salad greens, sliced cucumber, red onion, cherry tomatoes, chopped cilantro, and chopped mint.
  4. Slice Chicken: Slice grilled chicken breasts into thin strips.
  5. Assemble Salad: Add sliced chicken to the salad bowl.
  6. Serve: Divide salad among serving plates. Drizzle with plain yogurt and serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Tips:

  • You can use store-bought tikka masala paste or make your own by blending together spices with a little tomato paste.
  • For a vegetarian option, you can substitute chicken with paneer or tofu and follow the same marinade and grilling process.
  • Feel free to customize the salad with your favorite vegetables or add-ons like avocado, bell peppers, or toasted nuts.
  • Serve the salad with warm naan bread or pita for a complete meal.
